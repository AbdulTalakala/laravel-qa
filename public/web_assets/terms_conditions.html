Once a customer upgrades his package, he will be charged the difference amount of the package and will begin receiving the services as requested. Should a customer choose to go from Premium to Regular during the month, the change in his service offering will occur only at the start of the next month. No refunds will be made in this condition.

CANCELATION OF THE SERVICES.

Should the customer wish to cancel the subscription they may do so via the application provided. Customers will continue to receive the services until the end of the month. No refunds will be provided to the customer on cancelation of the service / subscriptions. Should a customer want their information completely removed from the system, the request should be provided in writing either physically or via email. It is recommended that all cancelations take place at least 48 hours before the renewal date in order to give due time anD reference to any third-party payment gateways to cancel the subscription plan provided.

REFUND POLICY

Customers of Swoop Parking Car washing LLC are subjected to a full refund on the amount paid as a “HOLD” amount when the user makes a selection of “2 Free Car washes” should the user cancel the free washes, they will be entitled to 2 free washes and a full refund on the amount paid for the subscription that will be calculated on a Pro rata basis for the remainder of the month. The Request to cancel should be processed within 7 days of activating the free washes. The User will receive an email inferring that they will be charged the remainder subscription amount after the 7 days of activating the subscription, should the user cancel within the 7 days, they will be entitled to a full refund. Failure to cancel will be considered as a willingness on the customers part to continue with the services and the customer will be charged and be provided the service for the remainder of the month based on the package and number of due washes. No Refunds will be made in any condition other than the condition mentioned above.

All refunds are processed by a third-party organization, CC avenue (Avenues World FC LLC). Swoop parking car washing LLC do not in any way process any online payments and as such it is the duty of the customer to inform Swoop Parking Car Washing LLC of any failure to receive their requested refund.
